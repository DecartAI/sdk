SEI Glass-to-Glass Loopback Proof

Pure browser proof that JavaScript can read and write H.264 SEI. A canvas (no camera) is encoded to H.264 and sent over a local RTCPeerConnection loopback. On the sender, a RTCRtpScriptTransform worker injects an SEI NAL stamped with Date.now(); on the receiver, the worker parses it back and reports glass-to-glass latency. No server, no SDK signaling.
